About

Background

My love for full stack web development comes from my desire to connect people through their shared interests. NYC is a big place but many people feel lonely. Why? People just need a common interest. My time in the New York Code + Design Academy Software Engineering Intensive has given me the skills needed to accomplish this goal and solve problems with code.

Strengths

I am strong in back-end development, namely using Rails in combination with JavaScript. I also excel in creating normalized, relational database schemas. For front-end development I take advantage of the Bootstrap front-end framework to facilitate my apps' appearance on different viewports and taught myself React.js to create reusable, stateful page components.
